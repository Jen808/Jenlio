Objectives

Operating systems across different platforms, including phones, computers, tablets, and car systems, are evolving rapidly to meet the increasing demands of users. These advancements significantly reduce user inconvenience and save time. Given the lower demand for flights compared to other modes of transportation, it is imperative to enhance the facilities within the aviation industry to attract more customers.

This project aims to enrich the in-flight entertainment (IFE) system, thereby elevating the overall travel experience. Drawing upon feedback from customers of various airlines, this initiative seeks to address their needs and concerns, minimizing inconvenience while maximizing convenience
