Objectives

This project centers on the redevelopment of GLANSIS, an initiative by NOAA, for a variety of significant reasons. It encompasses numerous categories and manages extensive datasets. Rochelle Sturtevant, our client, visited our team to share insights about their data, organizational structure, and specific needs. The project heavily relied on supporting materials from their existing website for research purposes.

The primary focus of this redesign was on enhancing the Educator Hub.

Rochelle Sturtevant, Ph.D., serves as the GLANSIS Program Manager at Michigan Sea Grant, affiliated with Michigan State University Extension and the NOAA Great Lakes Environmental Research Laboratory.
