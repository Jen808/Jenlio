Objectives

This project facilitates interaction between street vendors and customers, addressing challenges exacerbated by COVID-19, which reduced street vendor availability and complicated research efforts. The objective is to streamline the process for customers to locate vendors from home and for sellers to increase product sales through the app. Surveys and interviews with vendors and customers identified key needs: precise information on location, operating dates, and product pricing.
