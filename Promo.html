Promotional Materials

As a Marketing and Media Assistant, my primary responsibilities encompass two core areas. Firstly, I manage Social Media Engagement across platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. Secondly, I am tasked with the creation of Promotional Materials for CHS, including but not limited to materials for our nine programs and partners. Over the course of a year, I have successfully produced over 40 distinct items, ranging from social media graphics, posters, and booklets to advertisements for digital displays, newspapers, flyers, videos, and signage. Additionally, I actively participate in program events to capture photographic content.
