Objectives
  • Simplify Price Comparisons: Provide real-time cost breakdowns, including taxes and shipping.
  • Build Trust: Ensure seller and product authenticity with verification and reviews.
  • Save Time: Minimize effort in finding the best deals across platforms.
  • Enhance Usability: Offer a clean, easy-to-navigate interface.
  • Boost Confidence: Help users make informed decisions with clear, detailed information.
User Research
Hi-Fi Prototype
Home
The Home Page offers users an intuitive interface to explore products. It showcases personalized sections like "Deals from Your Favorite Sellers," "Weekly Most Clicked Items," and "Recommended for You." Users can seamlessly toggle between categories, search for items, and access key deals, all while enjoying a clean, visually engaging layout. The focus is on creating a personalized and efficient browsing experience.
Search Filter
The Search and Filter functionality allows users to refine their search effortlessly. With smart filters for price, brand, customer ratings, and benefit programs, users can quickly find the best deals. The search bar includes auto-suggestions and categories to improve discovery, ensuring users save time while locating their desired products.
Product Listing Page
The Product Detail Page provides a comprehensive view of each product, featuring high-quality images, detailed descriptions, and user reviews. Transparent pricing shows the full cost, including taxes and shipping, alongside comparison tools that highlight pricing across multiple platforms. Trust indicators, like seller verification badges, add credibility to the shopping experience.
